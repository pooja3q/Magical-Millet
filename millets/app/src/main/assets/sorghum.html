  • Gluten free food - Recommended for gluten intolerant and celiac patients.
  • Relatively low glycemic index and low glycemic load food - reduces the risk of diabetes mellitus
  • Invisible fat - lowers low-density lipoprotein (LDL) and improves high-density lipoprotein (HDL) cholesterol
  • Low calorie food - beneficial for obesity, diabetes and CVD
  • Rich source of antioxidants and polyphenols – reduces oxidative stress and carcinogenic properties
  • Bile acid and steroid binding properties - beneficial for obesity and CVD
  • Good Source of Nitrilosides – salicylates - fights against arthritis and rheumatism
  • Rich in dietary fiber - Beneficial for obesity, diabetes, CVD and inflammatory bowel disease and acts as a detoxifying agent
